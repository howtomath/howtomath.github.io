How to Solve for X

Step 1: Understand the Equation

Let's say you have a basic algebra equation like: 2x + 3 = 11. Your goal is to isolate x.

Step 2: Subtract to Simplify

Subtract 3 from both sides of the equation to remove the constant on the left side:

2x + 3 - 3 = 11 - 3

This simplifies to:

2x = 8

Step 3: Divide to Solve

Now divide both sides by 2:

2x / 2 = 8 / 2

Which gives you:

x = 4
